An Open Database of Covid Tenders and Innovations

The Coronavirus creates urgent demand for specialized technologies in airports, quarantine zones, hospitals, logistic supply, prisons,… Companies have been coming up with solutions that can be replicated in other countries, granted that they can match and talk to the buyers directly.

We want to facilitate the exchange of technology by opening up an open, public database of COVID-19 innovations. Suppliers of tech solutions can directly post their details online, and be openly searched and browsed by anyone who has a demand, in a peer-to-peer fashion.
